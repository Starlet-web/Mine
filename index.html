🧑‍🔬 Scholar I need to review some core ecology concepts today. Let's start with energy flow. Also, ensure terms like endosymbiosis are accurate as I am tying this into my M.Sc. Zoology dissertation research.
🤖 AI Mentor Absolutely. In any ecosystem, energy flow is strictly unidirectional, governed by the 10% law from producers to top consumers. 🧑‍🔬 Scholar Great. What is a quick summary of population strategies like r and K selection?
🤖 AI Mentor Here is a concise comparison for your review:

r-selection:
• High reproductive rate, rapid development.
• Thrive in unstable environments (e.g., weeds, insects).

K-selection:
• Low reproductive rate, high parental care.
• Thrive in stable environments near carrying capacity (e.g., large mammals).
